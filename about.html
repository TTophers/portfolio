Christopher Staples

Colorado-based web developer, SEO specialist, and all-around tinkerer. I create sleek websites, solve online problems, and help others navigate the digital world.

Early Life & Childhood

Grew up in Aurora, Colorado. I had a great childhood with my family—biking every day, hanging with friends, going to the movie theater, playing basketball and lacrosse. Until freshman year, life was mostly on autopilot.

From a young age, I was always playing video games, making sounds, taking apart printers and putting them back together. Music has always been a huge part of my life.

Early Coding Curiosity

I’ve always been into tinkering and experimenting. Platforms like Scratch got me hooked, and I spent hours in command tools seeing code change websites before I even refreshed them. I never thought of it as coding—I just loved seeing how things worked.

Learning Web Development

I went to DevSlops Coding Academy and then took the internet as my classroom, studying other people’s websites and learning by doing. I’ve always been shooting for gold—making drop-shipping sites that earned some money and pushing myself further.

Helping people and taking care of their online presence is what drives me. Every project is a chance to make someone’s life easier online.

SEO & Online Business

In 2022, after graduating, I realized how achievable online income can be if you work hard. I specialize in helping older folks navigate technology and manage their online presence. It’s rewarding to see the impact of my work and how it simplifies life for people who might struggle with tech.

Work Philosophy

My days are balanced. I’ll work on a website, then make some music, then spend time with family or go outside. Life is about flow and enjoying the work you do.

Design & coding philosophy: I focus on clean, intuitive, user-friendly design that looks as good as it works. Simplicity, functionality, and polish are key.

Passions & Hobbies

I ride BMX at a pro level and drift cars (350Z, Silvia line). Music is a huge part of my life—I make hip-hop and EDM. Outdoors are where I recharge: hiking, camping, and exploring in my beloved FourRunner.

Why I Do This

I love helping older folks with tech. I like being their personal little scientist, solving problems and guiding them through the digital world. Success isn’t just money—it’s happiness. If my clients are satisfied and happy, we both got paid.
